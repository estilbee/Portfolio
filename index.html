About Me

Olivia Daouphars is a floral designer turned software engineer. Currently living in Fairmount, Philadelphia, Olivia spends some of her free time at the Philadelphia Museum of Art and enjoys drawing at local coffee shops. Olivia is an imaginative thinker who is always seeking out new ways to learn. She found herself being pulled towards the coding world and was excited by the industry's ever expanding nature.
